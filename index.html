About Me

I'm a software developer, and I love creating things and solving problems. I got into coding when I was young by using interfaces and writing scripts to program bots to play my video games for me.

I built my first C++ program, a Blackjack game, in high school, and at UC Berkeley, I majored in stats, doing data analysis programming in R. I've since obsessed and dedicated myself to returning to my creative side and learning web development. I self-taught Ruby and Rails and evolved my understanding of the web, from OOP through MVC designs.

Most recently, I completed App Academy, a 12-week immersive software development program focusing on full-stack and agile web development with technologies such as Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, and Backbone.js. I'm looking for a cool team to work with and opportunities to learn more!
