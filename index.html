About Me

I'm a San Francisco based developer, and I love immersing myself in code and having full control over my product.

My first introduction to programming was in a summer robotics program. It was so cool being able to tell a little lego robot what to do from a computer interface! From there, I built my first C++ program, a Blackjack game in high school, and in college, I dove into data analysis programming in R.

Most recently, I attended App Academy, a 12-week immersive software development program focusing on full-stack and agile web development with technologies such as Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, and Backbone.js. I'm looking for opportunities to learn more!

Check out my projects. Feel free to contact me if you like what you see!
